About

tZERO, the global leader in blockchain technology for capital markets, aims to enable the market to securely tokenize and trade digitally enhanced securities. tZERO’s blockchain technologies aim to revolutionize the market and fix the inherent inefficiencies of Wall Street so that financial processes are less beholden to traditional, institutional market structures.

Vision

tZERO’s vision is to strengthen capital markets through the use of technology, with a primary focus on blockchain technology.
